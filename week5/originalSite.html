It's the first thing people notice, right off the bat if a webpage is faulty or poorly designed. Which is why it's extremely important to business owners, small and large to have a beautiful web page to display to customers and viewers. I just hope to have a good enough basic understanding of dreamweaver and web design to be able to do my own projects.
